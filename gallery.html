Bio

Modern folk with a storyteller's pulse

Ryan McKenzie pairs percussive acoustic guitar with honest, conversational lyrics. The set moves from intimate ballads to driving rhythms, keeping audiences engaged without overpowering the room.

Expect original songs with a few familiar favorites woven in, delivered with the same care whether it's a seated listening room or a busy taproom.

What You'll Get

A reliable, low-lift booking

  • Adjustable set lengths and volume to match your schedule.
  • Professional communication before, during, and after the show.
  • Original material plus tasteful covers tailored to the crowd.
  • Compact stage footprint that fits tight corners or small risers.
